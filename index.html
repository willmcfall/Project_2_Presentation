Application Problem Statement

Existing job posting sites assume that users are aware of jobs that would fit their personality. However, there are many individuals who are unclear of what their personality type is and which jobs are best suited for their personality.

Application Use Cases

  • Students interested in understanding careers that may fit their personality
  • Working professionals interested in changing careers and finding a job more naturally suited to their personality

Application Tools

    Bootstrap, Handlebars
  • Backend:

  • General:
    Git, Slack, VSCode, Reveal.JS, ESLint
